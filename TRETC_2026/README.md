## Core Framework: Five Learning Economies

The presentation introduces a taxonomy of **five fundamental Learning Economies** that represent distinct modes of value creation through knowledge acquisition:

1. **Breadth** - Multi-subject mastery and parallel domain competency
2. **Depth** - Time-to-competence optimization and acceleration
3. **Subset** - Career mobility through rapid retraining
4. **Superset** - Field reinvention and creative destruction
5. **Network-form** - Multi-scale knowledge routing and synthesis

**Key Concept:** **Resonance** - the universal transfer mechanism enabling cross-domain knowledge flow and synthesis across all five economies.

## Key Concepts

### The Five Learning Economies in Detail

**Breadth Economy:**
- Multi-subject/multi-degree mastery
- Portfolio breadth as new metric
- Parallel intellectual pipelines
- Metrics: diversity index, parallel domains, time to proficiency

**Depth Economy:**
- Acceleration to mastery
- Time-to-competence optimization
- AI scaffolding removes bottlenecks
- Metrics: shortest time to PhD-level mastery, hours to independent problem-solving

**Subset Economy:**
- Vocational/career re-training
- Real-time vocational retraining
- Career pivots compressed to months/weeks
- **Example:** ASU Microcredentials model
- Metrics: distinct fields mastered, retraining time, career pivot success rate

**Superset Economy:**
- Field reinvention/obviation
- Creative destruction of entire fields
- Historical examples: Jacquard loom, GPS, digital photography
- Metrics: fields obsoleted, time to replacement, economic impact

**Network-form Economy:**
- Multi-scale knowledge routing
- Connecting micro-details to macro-patterns
- AI optimizes cross-scale connections
- Metrics: scale traversals, compression ratio, emergent property discovery

### Resonance: The Universal Transfer Mechanism

- **Subset Mobility:** Skills transfer between domains (e.g., statistical thinking: physics â†’ finance â†’ marketing)
- **Superset Disruption:** Synthesis across domains creates field reinvention (e.g., digital photography: electronics + optics + chemistry)

**AI amplifies Resonance by:**
- Making cross-domain patterns more visible
- Surfacing non-obvious transfer opportunities
- Enabling synthesis across domains and scales

### AI as Learning Accelerator

**What AI Does:**
- Accelerates existing Learning Economy principles (10-100x speed increase)
- Provides differential capabilities: speed, scale, pattern recognition
- Optimizes multi-scale granule routing
- Generates dynamic pathways between knowledge domains

**What AI Doesn't Do:**
- Create fundamentally new learning economies
- Replace human cross-domain pattern recognition
- Eliminate need for meta-competencies
