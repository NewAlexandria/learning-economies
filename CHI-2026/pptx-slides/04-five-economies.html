The Five Learning Economies

  • Breadth — Multi-domain diversification; diversity index, parallel pipelines
  • Depth — Acceleration to mastery; time-to-competence, retention
  • Subset — Rapid retraining / mobility; transfer speed, career pivots
  • Superset — Field reinvention; cross-domain synthesis
  • Network-form — Multi-scale routing; scale traversals, topology

Resonance = transfer mechanism across all five (cross-domain knowledge flow)
