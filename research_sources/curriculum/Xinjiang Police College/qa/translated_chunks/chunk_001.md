EN: (6) Before bid opening, suppliers must configure their computer browser in advance (360 Browser or Google Chrome is recommended). The decryption time for response documents in this project is set to 30 minutes; if decryption cannot be completed normally due to the supplier’s own reasons, the consequences shall be borne by the supplier.

<!-- id: p000064 type: paragraph -->

EN: (7) On the day response documents are opened, suppliers do not need to arrive at the bid opening site; they only need to complete interactive steps such as remote decryption, bid opening announcement, inquiry and clarification, online multi-round quotation, and result publication through the “non-face-to-face” bid opening hall on the Government Procurement Cloud platform. Suppliers must use a “CA token” that can correctly decrypt the response documents to complete remote decryption within the prescribed time; if the supplier fails to decrypt, decryption fails, or decryption times out, it shall be deemed that the supplier has withdrawn its response document.
