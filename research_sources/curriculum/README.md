# Cybersecurity and OT Security Training Program Curricula

This directory contains curriculum information for various cybersecurity and Operational Technology (OT) security training programs.

## Programs Included

### Academic Degree Programs

1. **Georgetown University - Master of Professional Studies in Information Technology Management**
   - File: `georgetown-university-itm-cybersecurity.md`
   - Focus: IT Management with Cybersecurity specialization option
   - Format: Online and On-Campus

2. **Georgia Institute of Technology - Online Master of Science in Cybersecurity**
   - File: `georgia-tech-oms-cybersecurity.md`
   - Focus: Cybersecurity with Cyber-Physical Systems track (OT-relevant)
   - Format: Fully Online

### Research and Training Centers

3. **Mohawk College - Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT) Research Centre**
   - File: `mohawk-college-iiot.md`
   - Focus: Applied research in Industrial IoT
   - Type: Research Centre (not a traditional academic program)

### Professional Training Programs

4. **SANS Institute - OT Security Training**
   - File: `sans-ot-security-training.md`
   - Focus: Industrial Control Systems (ICS) and OT Security training
   - Type: Professional training and certification programs
   - Key Courses: ICS410, ICS515, GICSP certification

### Additional Sources (Reference / Translated)

5. **Xinjiang Police College - Cyberspace Security Comprehensive Laboratory Construction Project (Third Tender)**
   - File: `xinjiang-police-college-cyberspace-security-lab-en.md`
   - Source: （审定稿）新疆警察学院网络空间安全综合实验室建设项目（三次）.docx (Chinese government procurement bidding document)
   - Focus: Cyberspace security and cybersecurity-and-law-enforcement lab build: teaching platform, courses, attack-defense/CTF training, AI course, faculty training, hardware and integration
   - Type: Translated reference for strategy comparison (Chinese vs US cybersecurity education breadth); see `ingestion/WIP US overcome chinese edcation strategy.md`

## OT Security Relevance

### Programs with Direct OT Security Focus
- **Georgia Tech OMSCS - Cyber-Physical Systems Track**: Specifically addresses industrial control systems, critical infrastructure, and IoT device security

### Programs with OT Security Components
- **Georgetown ITM - Cybersecurity Focus Area**: Includes courses on security architecture and risk management applicable to OT environments
- **Mohawk College IIoT Research Centre**: Works with industrial IoT systems and sensor technologies relevant to OT security
