# Georgetown University - Master of Professional Studies in Information Technology Management

## Program Overview
- **Institution**: Georgetown University School of Continuing Studies
- **Program**: Master of Professional Studies in Information Technology Management
- **Format**: Online and On-Campus options available
- **Total Credits**: 36 credits (12 courses)
- **Duration**: Flexible, full-time or part-time schedules
- **URL**: https://scs.georgetown.edu/programs/525/master-of-professional-studies-in-information-technology-management/curriculum

## Program Structure

### Core Courses (6 credits)
1. **MPIT 5000: Ethics in Technology Management**
2. **MPIT 7990: Capstone Course**

### Foundation Courses (18 credits)
1. **MPIT 5650: Strategic Planning & Financial Management**
2. **MPIT 5600: Introduction to IT Management**
3. **MPIT 5700: Requirement Analysis and System Design**
4. **MPIT 5750: Enterprise Modernization**
5. **MPIT 5800: Communication for IT Managers**
6. **MPIT 5850: IT and Data Governance Strategy**

### Elective Courses (12 credits)
Students can choose four courses across focus areas or specialize in one area. Specializing in Project Management, Business Intelligence, or Cybersecurity allows students to earn a corresponding graduate certificate.

#### Cybersecurity Focus Area Courses
1. **Information Security**
2. **Cybersecurity Governance Frameworks**
3. **Information Assurance and Risk Management**
4. **Security Architecture and Design**
5. **Information Security Laws and Regulatory Compliance**
6. **Communication Strategy for Information Security Professionals**
7. **Disruptive Technology and Organizational Change**

#### Other Focus Areas
- **Project Management**: Agile Project Management Fundamentals, Lean-Agile with Scrum and Kanban, Agile Frameworks for Lean Enterprises, DevOps Essentials
- **Business Intelligence**: Competitive Intelligence Organizational Design, Global Competitive Intelligence, Information Management for Competitive Intelligence, Business Intelligence, Big Data & Analytics
- **AI & Digital Technology**: Courses focus on leveraging AI, cloud computing, and mobile technologies for strategic innovation and governance

## Specializations
Students can earn graduate certificates by completing all four elective courses in:
- Project Management
- Business Intelligence
- Cybersecurity

## Notes
- Program offers flexibility for adult learners
- Both online and on-campus formats available
- Full-time or part-time schedules supported
